Joaquin Guzman Loera, also known as "El Chapo Guzman" is the leader of Mexico's most powerful drug cartel, the Sinaloas. According to Forbes, Guzman is a billionaire1 and the "biggest drug lord ever."3 He is Mexico's wanted man with a US State Department reward of up to $5 million for information leading to his arrest.2
